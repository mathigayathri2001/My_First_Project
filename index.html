How to make sandwich

Egg sandwich

Things needed for mixing

  • Two bread slices
  • Two Boiled egg
  • one spoon mayonesse
  • one tablespoon pepper
  • one tablespoon salt
  • one spoon onion or celeary

How to prepare

Mash the eggs and add mayonesse,pepper,salt,onion/celary. Mis everything together .

Take the bread slices and apply liitle bit mayonesse on one side of each bread

Put the egg mixes on one bread slice, and cover with other bread slice,

Cut in to slices,and ENJOY EGG SANDWICH
